Chuong 5: Chuoi Markov thoi gian lien tuc
Continuous-Time Markov Chains

1. Qua trinh Poisson (The Poisson Process)

Cau chuyen: Hay tuong tuong ban ngoi o quan cafe quan sat xe buyt den tram. Trung binh cu 10 phut co mot chuyen, nhung chinh xac khi nao thi xe den — ban khong biet truoc. Co khi 5 phut da co xe, co khi phai doi 15 phut. Day chinh la ban chat cua qua trinh Poisson: su kien xay ra “ngau nhien” theo thoi gian, voi mot toc do trung binh co dinh.

Trong chuong nay ta bat dau nghien cuu cac qua trinh thoi gian lien tuc (continuous-time processes), la cac ho \((X_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) gom cac bien ngau nhien duoc danh chi so boi \(\mathbb{R}_+\). Khac voi chuoi Markov roi rac (thoi gian nhay tai \(n = 0, 1, 2, \ldots\)), o day thoi gian chay lien tuc va qua trinh co the “nhay” vao bat ky luc nao.

Tom tat

Y tuong chinh: Qua trinh Poisson dem so su kien xay ra theo thoi gian. No la “vien gach nen tang” cho moi chuoi Markov lien tuc, giong nhu buoc di ngau nhien la nen tang cho chuoi Markov roi rac.

Dinh nghia (Qua trinh Poisson chuan — Standard Poisson process)

Qua trinh Poisson chuan (standard Poisson process) la qua trinh ngau nhien \((N_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) chi co buoc nhay \(+1\), va duong di (paths) khong doi giua cac buoc nhay. Tai thoi diem \(t\):

\[ N_t = \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \ind_{(T_k, \infty)}(t), \qquad t \in \mathbb{R}_+, \]

trong do \((T_k)_{k\geq 1}\) la day tang cac thoi diem nhay voi \(\lim_{k\to\infty} T_k = +\infty\).

\((N_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) thoa man:

  1. Gia so doc lap (independence of increments): voi moi \(0 \leq t_0 < t_1 < \cdots < t_n\), cac bien \(N_{t_1} - N_{t_0}, \ldots, N_{t_n} - N_{t_{n-1}}\) la doc lap.
  2. Gia so dung (stationarity of increments): \(N_{t+h} - N_{s+h}\) co cung phan phoi voi \(N_t - N_s\) voi moi \(h > 0\) va \(0 \leq s \leq t\).

Vi du doi thuong:

  • So cuoc goi den tong dai trong khoang thoi gian \([0, t]\).
  • So khach hang vao cua hang tu 8h sang den gio \(t\).
  • So hat phong xa phat ra boi mot mau chat trong thoi gian \(t\).

Dinh ly 9 — Phan phoi Poisson

Gia su qua trinh dem \((N_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) thoa man cac dieu kien (1) va (2). Khi do:

\[ \PP(N_t - N_s = k) = e^{-\lambda(t-s)} \frac{(\lambda(t-s))^k}{k!}, \qquad k \in \mathbb{N}, \quad 0 \leq s \leq t, \]

voi hang so \(\lambda > 0\).

Tham so \(\lambda > 0\) duoc goi la cuong do (intensity) cua qua trinh:

\[ \lambda := \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} \PP(N_h = 1). \]

Dac biet, \(N_t\) co phan phoi Poisson (Poisson distribution) voi tham so \(\lambda t\):

\[ \PP(N_t = k) = \frac{(\lambda t)^k}{k!}\, e^{-\lambda t}, \qquad t > 0. \]

Xac suat chuyen vi mo (infinitesimal transition probabilities):

\begin{align} \PP(N_{t+h} - N_t = 1) &\simeq \lambda h, \qquad h \to 0, \\ \PP(N_{t+h} - N_t = 0) &\simeq 1 - \lambda h, \qquad h \to 0. \end{align}

Dieu nay co nghia: trong khoang thoi gian “ngan” \([t, t+h]\), gia so \(N_{t+h} - N_t\) xu su nhu bien ngau nhien Bernoulli voi tham so \(\lambda h\).

Menh de: Phan phoi thoi gian nhay

Luat cua \(T_n\) co mat do \(t \mapsto \lambda^n e^{-\lambda t} \frac{t^{n-1}}{(n-1)!}\) tren \(\mathbb{R}_+\), \(n \geq 1\).

Thoi gian \(\tau_k := T_{k+1} - T_k\) giua cac buoc nhay tao thanh day cac bien ngau nhien doc lap cung phan phoi (i.i.d.) co phan phoi mu voi tham so \(\lambda > 0\):

\[ \PP(\tau_0 > t_0, \ldots, \tau_n > t_n) = e^{-\lambda(t_0 + \cdots + t_n)} = \prod_{k=0}^n e^{-\lambda t_k}, \]

va \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_k] = 1/\lambda\).

Vi du: Xe buyt den tram

Gia su xe buyt den tram theo qua trinh Poisson voi cuong do \(\lambda = 6\) chuyen/gio (trung binh 10 phut/chuyen).

Cau hoi 1: Xac suat khong co xe nao trong 15 phut?

\[ \PP(N_{0.25} = 0) = e^{-6 \times 0.25} = e^{-1.5} \approx 0.223. \]

Cau hoi 2: Xac suat co dung 2 xe trong 20 phut?

\[ \PP(N_{1/3} = 2) = e^{-2} \frac{2^2}{2!} = 2e^{-2} \approx 0.271. \]

Cau hoi 3: Thoi gian doi trung binh cho xe tiep theo?

\[ \mathbb{E}[\tau] = \frac{1}{\lambda} = \frac{1}{6} \text{ gio} = 10 \text{ phut}. \]

Tom tat

Qua trinh Poisson: gia so doc lap va dung, \(N_t \sim \text{Poisson}(\lambda t)\). Cuong do \(\lambda\) cao hon \(\Rightarrow\) nhay thuong xuyen hon \(\Rightarrow\) thoi gian o moi trang thai ngan hon (\(\mathbb{E}[\tau_k] = 1/\lambda\)).

Tinh chat “khong nho” (memoryless): du ban da doi 5 phut roi, thoi gian doi them van co phan phoi mu — qua khu khong anh huong tuong lai.

2. Qua trinh sinh-tu (Birth and Death Processes)

Cau chuyen: Hay tuong tuong mot quan cafe. Khach den (“sinh”) va khach di (“tu”). Neu quan dang co \(i\) khach:

  • Khach moi den voi toc do \(\lambda_i\) (so khach tang len 1).
  • Mot khach hien tai roi di voi toc do \(\mu_i\) (so khach giam di 1).

So khach trong quan thay doi lien tuc theo thoi gian — day la qua trinh sinh-tu.

Dinh nghia (Qua trinh sinh thuan tuy — Pure birth process)

Chuoi Markov thoi gian lien tuc \((X_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) thoa man:

\[ \PP(X_{t+h} - X_t = 1 \mid X_t = i) \simeq \lambda_i h, \qquad h \to 0,\; i \in S, \] \[ \PP(X_{t+h} - X_t = 0 \mid X_t = i) \simeq 1 - \lambda_i h, \qquad h \to 0,\; i \in S, \]

duoc goi la qua trinh sinh thuan tuy (pure birth process) voi toc do sinh \(\lambda_i \geq 0\). Qua trinh Poisson la truong hop dac biet voi \(\lambda_n = \lambda\) khong phu thuoc trang thai.

Dinh nghia (Qua trinh tu thuan tuy — Pure death process)

Tuong tu, \((X_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) voi:

\[ \PP(X_{t+h} - X_t = -1 \mid X_t = i) \simeq \mu_i h, \quad \PP(X_{t+h} - X_t = 0 \mid X_t = i) \simeq 1 - \mu_i h, \]

la qua trinh tu thuan tuy (pure death process) voi toc do tu \(\mu_i \geq 0\).

Dinh nghia (Qua trinh sinh-tu — Birth and death process)

Chuoi Markov thoi gian lien tuc \((X_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) voi:

\begin{align*} \PP(X_{t+h} - X_t = 1 \mid X_t = i) &\simeq \lambda_i h, \\ \PP(X_{t+h} - X_t = -1 \mid X_t = i) &\simeq \mu_i h, \\ \PP(X_{t+h} - X_t = 0 \mid X_t = i) &\simeq 1 - (\lambda_i + \mu_i)h, \end{align*}

khi \(h \to 0\), duoc goi la qua trinh sinh-tu (birth and death process) voi toc do sinh \(\lambda_i \geq 0\) va toc do tu \(\mu_i \geq 0\), \(i \in S\).

Thoi gian \(\tau_i\) o trang thai \(i\) truoc khi chuyen sang trang thai khac la bien ngau nhien co phan phoi mu:

\[ \tau_i = \min(\tau_{i,i+1},\; \tau_{i,i-1}), \]

voi tham so \(\lambda_i + \mu_i\), va \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_i] = \frac{1}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}\).

Vi du: Quan cafe co suc chua \(N = 4\)

Xet quan cafe co toi da \(4\) cho ngoi. Khach den voi toc do \(\lambda = 2\) nguoi/gio, khach roi di voi toc do \(\mu = 3\) nguoi/gio (moi khach ngoi trung binh 20 phut).

Khi quan co \(i\) khach (\(0 \leq i \leq 4\)):

  • Toc do sinh: \(\lambda_i = \lambda = 2\) (neu \(i < 4\)), \(\lambda_4 = 0\) (quan day).
  • Toc do tu: \(\mu_i = i \cdot \mu = 3i\) (moi khach roi di doc lap voi toc do \(\mu\)).

Thoi gian trung binh o trang thai \(i = 2\):

\[ \mathbb{E}[\tau_2] = \frac{1}{\lambda_2 + \mu_2} = \frac{1}{2 + 6} = \frac{1}{8} \text{ gio} = 7.5 \text{ phut}. \]

3. Chuoi Markov thoi gian lien tuc (Continuous-Time Markov Chains)

Cau chuyen: Giong nhu con kien tren ban co (Chuong 1) chi quan tam o hien tai, chuoi Markov lien tuc cung “khong nho” — nhung bay gio con kien di chuyen trong thoi gian lien tuc. No o mot o trong thoi gian ngau nhien (phan phoi mu), roi nhay sang o khac.

Dinh nghia (Tinh chat Markov lien tuc — Continuous-time Markov property)

Qua trinh ngau nhien \((X_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) nhan gia tri trong \(\mathbb{Z}\) co tinh chat Markov neu voi moi \(0 < s_1 < \cdots < s_{n-1} < s < t\):

\[ \PP(X_t = j \mid X_s = i_n,\; X_{s_{n-1}} = i_{n-1},\; \ldots,\; X_{s_1} = i_0) = \PP(X_t = j \mid X_s = i_n). \]

Vi du: Qua trinh Poisson la chuoi Markov thoi gian lien tuc vi no co gia so doc lap. Tong quat hon, moi qua trinh co gia so doc lap deu la chuoi Markov.

Nua nhom chuyen (Transition Semigroup)

Xac suat chuyen phu thuoc thoi gian:

\[ P_{i,j}(t) := \PP(X_{t+s} = j \mid X_s = i), \qquad i,j \in \mathbb{Z},\; t \in \mathbb{R}_+, \]

trong do ta gia su qua trinh la dong nhat theo thoi gian (time homogeneous): \(\PP(X_{t+s} = j \mid X_s = i)\) khong phu thuoc \(s\). Luu y \(P(0) = I_d\).

Ma tran \(P(t) = [P_{i,j}(t)]_{i,j\in S}\) duoc goi la nua nhom chuyen (transition semigroup).

Tinh chat nua nhom (semigroup property):

\[ P(s+t) = P(s)\,P(t) = P(t)\,P(s), \qquad s, t \in \mathbb{R}_+. \]

Day chinh la phuong trinh Chapman–Kolmogorov phien ban thoi gian lien tuc.

4. Ma tran sinh vo cung be (Infinitesimal Generator)

Cau chuyen: Trong truong hop roi rac, ma tran chuyen \(P\) cho ban biet “xac suat di dau sau 1 buoc”. Nhung trong thoi gian lien tuc, khong co “1 buoc” — thoi gian chay lien tuc! Thay vao do, ta can biet he thong thay doi nhanh the nao — day la vai tro cua ma tran sinh \(Q\).

Hay nghi \(Q\) nhu “toc do” thay vi “xac suat”:

  • \(\lambda_{i,j} > 0\) (ngoai duong cheo): toc do chuyen tu \(i\) sang \(j\). Cang lon \(\Rightarrow\) cang nhanh.
  • \(\lambda_{i,i} < 0\) (duong cheo): toc do roi khoi \(i\). \(|\lambda_{i,i}|\) lon \(\Rightarrow\) o \(i\) rat ngan.

Bang cach lay dao ham quan he nua nhom theo \(t\) roi cho \(t = 0\):

\[ P'(t) = \lim_{s \to 0} \frac{P(t+s) - P(t)}{s} = P(t) \lim_{s \to 0} \frac{P(s) - P(0)}{s} = P(t)\,Q, \]

trong do:

Dinh nghia (Ma tran sinh — Infinitesimal generator)

\[ Q := P'(0) = \lim_{s \to 0} \frac{P(s) - P(0)}{s} \]

duoc goi la ma tran sinh vo cung be (infinitesimal generator) cua \((X_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\).

Ky hieu \(Q = [\lambda_{i,j}]_{i,j\in S}\). Cac hang cua \(Q\) luon co tong bang 0:

\[ \sum_{j \in S} \lambda_{i,j} = 0, \qquad i \in S. \]

Do do: \(\lambda_{i,i} = -\sum_{l \neq i} \lambda_{i,l}\) (phan tu duong cheo luon am hoac bang 0).

Phuong trinh Kolmogorov tien (forward Kolmogorov equation):

\[ P'(t) = P(t)\,Q, \qquad t > 0. \]

Phuong trinh Kolmogorov lui (backward Kolmogorov equation):

\[ P'(t) = Q\,P(t), \qquad t > 0. \]

Nghiem (dung ham mu ma tran — matrix exponential):

\[ P(t) = P(0)\,e^{tQ} = e^{tQ}, \qquad \text{trong do } e^{tQ} = I_d + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{t^n}{n!} Q^n. \]

Xap xi bac nhat khi \(h \to 0\):

\[ P(h) \simeq I_d + hQ + o(h), \qquad h \to 0. \]

Xac suat chuyen vi mo:

\[ P_{i,j}(h) = \PP(X_{t+h} = j \mid X_t = i) \simeq \begin{cases} \lambda_{i,j} h, & i \neq j, \\[3pt] 1 + \lambda_{i,i} h, & i = j, \end{cases} \qquad h \to 0. \]

Y nghia vat ly: Qua trinh chuyen tu trang thai \(i\) sang \(j \neq i\) giong nhu qua trinh Poisson voi cuong do \(\lambda_{i,j}\). Thoi gian \(\tau_{i,j}\) truoc khi chuyen tu \(i\) sang \(j\) co phan phoi mu:

\[ \PP(\tau_{i,j} > t) = e^{-\lambda_{i,j} t}, \qquad t \in \mathbb{R}_+, \quad i \neq j. \]

va \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_{i,j}] = 1/\lambda_{i,j}\).

Thoi gian o trang thai \(i\) (truoc khi roi di bat ky dau):

\[ \tau_i = \min_{j \in S,\, j \neq i} \tau_{i,j}, \]

la bien mu voi tham so \(\sum_{j \neq i} \lambda_{i,j} = -\lambda_{i,i}\), nen \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_i] = -1/\lambda_{i,i}\).

Vi du cac ma tran sinh

Vi du: Qua trinh Poisson

Ma tran sinh:

\[ Q = [\lambda_{i,j}]_{i,j\in\mathbb{N}} = \begin{bmatrix} -\lambda & \lambda & 0 & \cdots \\ 0 & -\lambda & \lambda & \cdots \\ 0 & 0 & -\lambda & \cdots \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots \end{bmatrix}. \]

Thoi gian o moi trang thai: \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_i] = 1/\lambda\) (khong phu thuoc \(i\)).

Vi du: Qua trinh sinh-tu tren \(\{0,1,\ldots,N\}\)

Ma tran sinh (voi \(\mu_0 = \lambda_N = 0\)):

\[ Q = \begin{bmatrix} -\lambda_0 & \lambda_0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\ \mu_1 & -\lambda_1-\mu_1 & \lambda_1 & \cdots & 0 \\ \vdots & \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & \vdots \\ 0 & \cdots & \mu_{N-1} & -\lambda_{N-1}-\mu_{N-1} & \lambda_{N-1} \\ 0 & \cdots & 0 & \mu_N & -\mu_N \end{bmatrix}. \]

Vi du: Ma tran sinh 5 trang thai — tinh toan chi tiet

Xet qua trinh Markov lien tuc tren \(S = \{0, 1, 2, 3, 4\}\) voi ma tran sinh:

\[ Q = \begin{bmatrix} -3 & 2 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & -4 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 2 & -5 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 4 & -4 \end{bmatrix}. \]

Doc thong tin tu \(Q\):

  • Tu trang thai \(0\): chuyen sang \(1\) voi toc do \(2\), sang \(2\) voi toc do \(1\). Tong toc do roi = \(3\).
  • Thoi gian trung binh o trang thai \(0\): \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_0] = 1/3\) (don vi thoi gian).
  • Thoi gian trung binh o trang thai \(3\): \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_3] = 1/5\) (trang thai “ban ron” nhat).
  • Tu trang thai \(4\): chi co the quay lai \(3\) (toc do \(4\)), \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_4] = 1/4\).

Tom tat

Ma tran sinh \(Q\) dong vai tro tuong tu ma tran chuyen \(P\) trong truong hop roi rac. Quan he: \(P(h) \approx I_d + hQ\) cho \(h\) nho — “roi rac hoa” chuoi lien tuc. Khac biet chinh: phan tu ngoai duong cheo \(\lambda_{i,j} \geq 0\) (toc do), phan tu duong cheo \(\lambda_{i,i} \leq 0\).

So sanh \(P\) (roi rac) vs \(Q\) (lien tuc):

Ma tran \(P\) Ma tran \(Q\)
Phan tu ngoai duong cheo \(\geq 0\) (xac suat) \(\geq 0\) (toc do)
Phan tu duong cheo \(\geq 0\) \(\leq 0\)
Tong moi hang \(= 1\) \(= 0\)
Y nghia “di dau” “di nhanh the nao”

5. Chuoi Markov hai trang thai lien tuc (Two-State Continuous-Time Chain)

Xet qua trinh Markov lien tuc tren \(S = \{0, 1\}\) voi ma tran sinh:

\[ Q = \begin{bmatrix} -\alpha & \alpha \\ \beta & -\beta \end{bmatrix}, \qquad \alpha, \beta \geq 0. \]

Vi du: Mot may tinh co the o trang thai “hoat dong” (0) hoac “hong” (1). May hong voi toc do \(\alpha\) va duoc sua voi toc do \(\beta\).

Phuong trinh Kolmogorov tien \(P'(t) = P(t) Q\) cho he phuong trinh vi phan:

\[ \begin{cases} P'_{0,0}(t) = -\alpha P_{0,0}(t) + \beta P_{0,1}(t), \\ P'_{0,1}(t) = \alpha P_{0,0}(t) - \beta P_{0,1}(t), \end{cases} \]

voi dieu kien dau \(P(0) = I_d\).

Nghiem bang ham mu ma tran:

Ma tran \(Q\) co tri rieng \(\lambda_1 = 0\) va \(\lambda_2 = -(\alpha + \beta)\).

Cheo hoa va tinh \(e^{tQ}\):

\[ P(t) = e^{tQ} = \frac{1}{\alpha+\beta} \begin{bmatrix} \beta + \alpha e^{-t(\alpha+\beta)} & \alpha - \alpha e^{-t(\alpha+\beta)} \\ \beta - \beta e^{-t(\alpha+\beta)} & \alpha + \beta e^{-t(\alpha+\beta)} \end{bmatrix}, \quad t > 0. \]

So sanh voi truong hop roi rac: Bieu thuc tren tuong tu cong thuc roi rac (Chuong 1), nhung thay \(\lambda_2^n = (1-a-b)^n\) bang \(e^{-t(\alpha+\beta)}\). Su hoi tu theo ham mu thay vi luy thua.

Phan phoi gioi han

Khi \(t \to \infty\) (voi \(\alpha > 0\) hoac \(\beta > 0\)):

\begin{align} \lim_{t\to\infty} \PP(X_t = 1 \mid X_0 = 0) &= \lim_{t\to\infty} \PP(X_t = 1 \mid X_0 = 1) = \frac{\alpha}{\alpha+\beta}, \\ \lim_{t\to\infty} \PP(X_t = 0 \mid X_0 = 0) &= \lim_{t\to\infty} \PP(X_t = 0 \mid X_0 = 1) = \frac{\beta}{\alpha+\beta}. \end{align}

Phan phoi gioi han:

\[ \pi = (\pi_0, \pi_1) = \left(\frac{\beta}{\alpha+\beta},\; \frac{\alpha}{\alpha+\beta}\right). \]

Tom tat

Chuoi hai trang thai lien tuc co cung phan phoi gioi han \(\pi = (\beta/(\alpha+\beta),\; \alpha/(\alpha+\beta))\) nhu truong hop roi rac (thay \(a \to \alpha\), \(b \to \beta\)). Su hoi tu luon xay ra trong thoi gian lien tuc (tru \(\alpha = \beta = 0\)), va nhanh hon khi \(\alpha + \beta\) lon hon.

6. Phan phoi gioi han va dung (Limiting and Stationary Distributions)

Cau chuyen: Quay lai vi du quan cafe. Sau nhieu gio hoat dong, ty le thoi gian quan co \(0, 1, 2, \ldots\) khach se on dinh — khong phu thuoc vao luc mo cua co bao nhieu khach. Day chinh la phan phoi dung: “dan so on dinh” sau thoi gian dai.

Dinh nghia (Phan phoi dung lien tuc — Stationary distribution, continuous-time)

Phan phoi \(\pi = (\pi_i)_{i\in S}\) duoc goi la dung (stationary) neu \(\pi P(t) = \pi\) voi moi \(t \in \mathbb{R}_+\).

Menh de 4

Phan phoi \(\pi = (\pi_i)_{i\in S}\) la dung khi va chi khi:

\[ \pi Q = 0. \]

Chung minh. Neu \(\pi Q = 0\), thi \(\pi P(t) = \pi e^{tQ} = \pi \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{t^n}{n!} Q^n = \pi\). Nguoc lai, lay dao ham \(\pi = \pi P(t)\) tai \(t = 0\): \(0 = \pi P'(0) = \pi Q P(0) = \pi Q\). \(\square\)

Menh de 5

Neu chuoi Markov lien tuc \((X_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\) co phan phoi gioi han

\[ \pi_j := \lim_{t\to\infty} \PP(X_t = j \mid X_0 = i) = \lim_{t\to\infty} P_{i,j}(t), \qquad j \in S, \]

khong phu thuoc vao trang thai ban dau \(i \in S\), thi \(\pi Q = 0\), tuc \(\pi\) la phan phoi dung.

Tim phan phoi dung: Giai he \(\pi Q = 0\) ket hop \(\sum_i \pi_i = 1\).

Tuong duong: \(\sum_{i \in S} \pi_i \lambda_{i,j} = 0\) voi moi \(j \in S\).

Luu y: dieu kien \(\pi Q = 0\) tuong duong voi \(\pi = \pi(I_d + hQ)\) cho moi \(h > 0\), chinh la phan phoi dung cua chuoi roi rac co ma tran chuyen \(P(h) \simeq I_d + hQ\).

Phan phoi dung cua qua trinh sinh-tu

Voi qua trinh sinh-tu tren \(\mathbb{N}\) co ma tran sinh \(Q = [\lambda_{i,j}]\), he \(\pi Q = 0\) cho:

\[ \begin{cases} 0 = -\lambda_0 \pi_0 + \mu_1 \pi_1 \\ 0 = \lambda_{j-1}\pi_{j-1} - (\lambda_j + \mu_j)\pi_j + \mu_{j+1}\pi_{j+1}, \quad j \geq 1 \end{cases} \]

Giai de quy:

\[ \pi_1 = \frac{\lambda_0}{\mu_1}\pi_0, \qquad \pi_{j+1} = \frac{\lambda_j \cdots \lambda_0}{\mu_{j+1} \cdots \mu_1} \pi_0, \qquad j \geq 0, \]

voi \(\pi_0\) duoc xac dinh boi \(\sum_{i=0}^{\infty} \pi_i = 1\):

\[ \pi_0 = \frac{1}{\displaystyle\sum_{i=0}^{\infty} \frac{\lambda_{i-1} \cdots \lambda_0}{\mu_i \cdots \mu_1}}. \]

Truong hop dac biet: Khi \(\lambda_i = \lambda\) va \(\mu_i = \mu\) voi moi \(i \geq 1\):

\[ \pi_j = \left(1 - \frac{\lambda}{\mu}\right) \left(\frac{\lambda}{\mu}\right)^j, \qquad j \in \mathbb{N}, \]

voi dieu kien \(\lambda < \mu\) (phan phoi hinh hoc voi tham so \(\lambda/\mu\)).

Vi du: Hang doi M/M/1

Mot quay phuc vu co khach den theo Poisson voi toc do \(\lambda\) va thoi gian phuc vu mu voi toc do \(\mu\). Day la qua trinh sinh-tu voi \(\lambda_i = \lambda\) va \(\mu_i = \mu\) cho moi \(i \geq 1\), \(\mu_0 = 0\).

Neu \(\rho = \lambda/\mu < 1\) (he thong on dinh):

\[ \pi_j = (1 - \rho)\rho^j, \qquad j \in \mathbb{N}. \]

So khach trung binh trong he thong:

\[ \mathbb{E}[X] = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} j\, \pi_j = \frac{\rho}{1-\rho} = \frac{\lambda}{\mu - \lambda}. \]

Vi du: neu \(\lambda = 4\) khach/gio, \(\mu = 5\) khach/gio, thi \(\rho = 0.8\) va \(\mathbb{E}[X] = 4\) khach.

7. Chuoi nhung (The Embedded Chain)

Cau chuyen: Hay tuong tuong ban quay video quan cafe ca ngay. Chuoi Markov lien tuc la toan bo video. Nhung neu ban chi chup anh moi khi co su thay doi (khach den hoac di), ban thu duoc mot “bo anh” — day chinh la chuoi nhung.

Chuoi nhung “bo qua thoi gian”, chi ghi lai trang thai nao xuat hien, khong quan tam bao lau o moi trang thai.

Dinh nghia (Chuoi nhung — Embedded chain)

Xet day \((T_n)_{n\in\mathbb{N}}\) cac thoi diem nhay cua qua trinh Markov lien tuc \((X_t)_{t\in\mathbb{R}_+}\):

\[ T_1 = \inf\{t > 0 : X_t \neq X_0\}, \qquad T_{n+1} = \inf\{t > T_n : X_t \neq X_{T_n}\}, \quad n \in \mathbb{N}. \]

Chuoi Markov nhung (embedded Markov chain) la chuoi roi rac \((Z_n)_{n\in\mathbb{N}}\) voi:

\[ Z_0 = X_0, \qquad Z_n = X_{T_n}, \quad n \in \mathbb{N}. \]

Ma tran chuyen cua chuoi nhung

Voi qua trinh sinh-tu, ma tran chuyen cua chuoi nhung:

\[ P_{i,i+1} = \frac{\lambda_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}, \qquad P_{i,i-1} = \frac{\mu_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}, \qquad i \in \mathbb{N}. \]

Dieu nay la do:

\[ \PP(\tau_{i,i+1} < \tau_{i,i-1}) = \frac{\lambda_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}, \]

theo tinh chat canh tranh giua hai bien mu doc lap.

Tong quat: Voi chuoi Markov lien tuc co ma tran sinh \(Q = [\lambda_{i,j}]\), ma tran chuyen cua chuoi nhung la:

\[ \hat{P}_{i,j} = \frac{\lambda_{i,j}}{-\lambda_{i,i}} = \frac{\lambda_{i,j}}{\sum_{l \neq i} \lambda_{i,l}}, \qquad i \neq j. \]

Vi du: Tu ma tran \(Q\) den chuoi nhung — 5 trang thai

Xet qua trinh o Vi du ma tran sinh 5 trang thai voi ma tran sinh:

\[ Q = \begin{bmatrix} -3 & 2 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & -4 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 2 & -5 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 4 & -4 \end{bmatrix}. \]

Ma tran chuyen cua chuoi nhung \(\hat{P}\):

\[ \hat{P} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2/3 & 1/3 & 0 & 0 \\ 1/4 & 0 & 2/4 & 1/4 & 0 \\ 0 & 1/3 & 0 & 1/3 & 1/3 \\ 0 & 0 & 2/5 & 0 & 3/5 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}. \]

Cach tinh: Hang \(i\) cua \(\hat{P}\) = hang \(i\) cua \(Q\) (bo phan tu duong cheo), chia cho \(|\lambda_{i,i}|\).

Vi du hang 1 (trang thai 1): \(\lambda_{1,0} = 1\), \(\lambda_{1,2} = 2\), \(\lambda_{1,3} = 1\), \(|\lambda_{1,1}| = 4\).

\[ \hat{P}_{1,0} = \frac{1}{4}, \quad \hat{P}_{1,2} = \frac{2}{4}, \quad \hat{P}_{1,3} = \frac{1}{4}. \]

Tom tat

Chuoi nhung “roi rac hoa” qua trinh lien tuc bang cach chi ghi lai cac trang thai tai thoi diem nhay. Ma tran chuyen cua chuoi nhung chi phu thuoc vao ty le cac toc do, khong phu thuoc vao toc do tuyet doi.

De phuc hoi qua trinh lien tuc tu chuoi nhung, can them thong tin ve thoi gian o moi trang thai \(\tau_i \sim \text{Exp}(-\lambda_{i,i})\).

8. Xac suat hap thu (Absorption Probabilities)

Cau chuyen: Hay tuong tuong mot con kien bat dau o vi tri \(i\) tren mot thanh go. Hai dau thanh go (\(0\) va \(N\)) la “tuong” — khi cham tuong, kien dung lai vinh vien. Cau hoi: “Con kien se cham tuong nao? Voi xac suat bao nhieu?”

Day la bai toan xac suat hap thu cho qua trinh sinh-tu lien tuc. Vi chuoi nhung quyet dinh “di dau” (khong quan tam “bao lau”), xac suat hap thu cua qua trinh lien tuc bang xac suat hap thu cua chuoi nhung.

Thiet lap bai toan

Xet qua trinh sinh-tu tren \(S = \{0, 1, \ldots, N\}\) voi:

  • Trang thai \(0\) va \(N\) la hap thu: \(\lambda_0 = 0\) (khong sinh khi o 0) va \(\mu_N = 0\) (khong tu khi o \(N\)).
  • Khi o trang thai \(i\) (\(1 \leq i \leq N-1\)): sinh voi toc do \(\lambda_i\), tu voi toc do \(\mu_i\).

Dat \(g_0(i) = \PP(\text{bi hap thu vao trang thai } 0 \mid X_0 = i)\).

Phuong trinh cho xac suat hap thu

Vi xac suat hap thu chi phu thuoc chuoi nhung, ta ap dung phan tich buoc dau tien tren chuoi nhung:

\[ g_0(i) = \hat{P}_{i,i-1}\, g_0(i-1) + \hat{P}_{i,i+1}\, g_0(i+1), \qquad 1 \leq i \leq N-1, \]

voi dieu kien bien \(g_0(0) = 1\) va \(g_0(N) = 0\).

Thay \(\hat{P}_{i,i-1} = \frac{\mu_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}\) va \(\hat{P}_{i,i+1} = \frac{\lambda_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}\):

\[ g_0(i) = \frac{\mu_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}\, g_0(i-1) + \frac{\lambda_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}\, g_0(i+1). \]

Day la phuong trinh “con bac” (gambler's equation) o dang lien tuc.

Nghiem tuong minh

Dat \(\rho_i = \mu_i / \lambda_i\) (ty so tu/sinh). Viet lai:

\[ g_0(i) - g_0(i-1) = \frac{\lambda_i}{\mu_i}\left[g_0(i+1) - g_0(i)\right], \]

hay:

\[ g_0(i+1) - g_0(i) = \rho_i \left[g_0(i) - g_0(i-1)\right]. \]

Dat \(d_i = g_0(i) - g_0(i-1)\). Ta co \(d_{i+1} = \rho_i\, d_i\), suy ra:

\[ d_k = d_1 \prod_{j=1}^{k-1} \rho_j = d_1 \prod_{j=1}^{k-1} \frac{\mu_j}{\lambda_j}, \qquad k \geq 2. \]

Tu \(g_0(0) = 1\) va \(g_0(N) = 0\):

\[ g_0(N) - g_0(0) = \sum_{k=1}^{N} d_k = -1. \]

Dat \(R_k = \prod_{j=1}^{k-1} \frac{\mu_j}{\lambda_j}\) (voi \(R_1 = 1\)). Ta duoc \(d_1 \sum_{k=1}^{N} R_k = -1\), nen:

Xac suat hap thu vao trang thai \(0\):

\[ g_0(i) = \frac{\displaystyle\sum_{k=i+1}^{N} R_k}{\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{N} R_k}, \qquad \text{voi } R_k = \prod_{j=1}^{k-1} \frac{\mu_j}{\lambda_j},\; R_1 = 1. \]

Truong hop dac biet \(\lambda_i = \lambda\), \(\mu_i = \mu\) (toc do khong doi):

Neu \(\rho = \mu/\lambda \neq 1\): \(R_k = \rho^{k-1}\), suy ra:

\[ g_0(i) = \frac{\rho^i - \rho^N}{1 - \rho^N}. \]

Neu \(\rho = 1\) (tuc \(\lambda = \mu\)):

\[ g_0(i) = 1 - \frac{i}{N}. \]

Vi du: Xac suat hap thu voi \(N = 4\)

Xet qua trinh sinh-tu tren \(\{0, 1, 2, 3, 4\}\) voi \(\lambda_i = 2\), \(\mu_i = 3\) cho \(1 \leq i \leq 3\).

Ta co \(\rho = \mu/\lambda = 3/2\). Ap dung cong thuc:

\[ g_0(i) = \frac{(3/2)^i - (3/2)^4}{1 - (3/2)^4} = \frac{(3/2)^i - 81/16}{1 - 81/16} = \frac{(3/2)^i - 81/16}{-65/16}. \]

Tinh cu the:

\begin{align*} g_0(1) &= \frac{3/2 - 81/16}{-65/16} = \frac{24/16 - 81/16}{-65/16} = \frac{-57/16}{-65/16} = \frac{57}{65} \approx 0.877, \\ g_0(2) &= \frac{9/4 - 81/16}{-65/16} = \frac{36/16 - 81/16}{-65/16} = \frac{45}{65} = \frac{9}{13} \approx 0.692, \\ g_0(3) &= \frac{27/8 - 81/16}{-65/16} = \frac{54/16 - 81/16}{-65/16} = \frac{27}{65} \approx 0.415. \end{align*}

Nhan xet: Vi \(\mu > \lambda\) (toc do tu > toc do sinh), qua trinh “keo ve 0” manh hon, nen xac suat cham 0 kha cao ngay ca tu trang thai 3.

9. Thoi gian hap thu trung binh (Mean Absorption Times)

Cau chuyen: Ta da biet con kien se cham tuong. Nhung mat bao lau? Trong thoi gian lien tuc, day la cau hoi quan trong vi thoi gian co y nghia thuc te (vi du: “Trung binh bao lau thi he thong tat?”).

Khac voi chuoi roi rac (dem so buoc), o day ta can cong cac thoi gian luu tru (sojourn times) tai moi trang thai.

Thiet lap

Dat \(\eta(i) = \mathbb{E}[T_{\text{abs}} \mid X_0 = i]\), trong do \(T_{\text{abs}}\) la thoi gian den khi bi hap thu (cham \(0\) hoac \(N\)).

Voi qua trinh sinh-tu, phan tich buoc dau tien tren chuoi nhung cho:

\[ \eta(i) = \mathbb{E}[\tau_i] + \hat{P}_{i,i-1}\, \eta(i-1) + \hat{P}_{i,i+1}\, \eta(i+1), \qquad 1 \leq i \leq N-1, \]

voi dieu kien bien \(\eta(0) = \eta(N) = 0\).

O day \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_i] = \frac{1}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}\) la thoi gian trung binh o trang thai \(i\) truoc khi nhay.

Su khac biet so voi roi rac: Trong truong hop roi rac, ta co “\(+1\)” (moi buoc mat 1 don vi thoi gian). O day, ta thay bang “\(+\mathbb{E}[\tau_i]\)” — moi buoc mat thoi gian \(\text{Exp}(\lambda_i + \mu_i)\).

Nghiem

Thay \(\hat{P}_{i,i\pm 1}\) va \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_i]\) vao:

\[ \eta(i) = \frac{1}{\lambda_i + \mu_i} + \frac{\mu_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}\, \eta(i-1) + \frac{\lambda_i}{\lambda_i + \mu_i}\, \eta(i+1). \]

Nhan ca hai ve voi \((\lambda_i + \mu_i)\):

\[ (\lambda_i + \mu_i)\eta(i) = 1 + \mu_i\, \eta(i-1) + \lambda_i\, \eta(i+1). \]

Viet lai:

\[ \lambda_i[\eta(i) - \eta(i+1)] = 1 + \mu_i[\eta(i-1) - \eta(i)]. \]

Dat \(\Delta_i = \eta(i) - \eta(i+1)\). Ta co quan he de quy:

\[ \lambda_i \Delta_i = 1 - \mu_i \Delta_{i-1}, \]

hay:

\[ \Delta_i = \frac{1}{\lambda_i} - \frac{\mu_i}{\lambda_i} \Delta_{i-1} = \frac{1}{\lambda_i} + \rho_i \Delta_{i-1}. \]

Vi du: Thoi gian hap thu trung binh voi \(N = 4\)

Tiep tuc Vi du xac suat hap thu voi N=4 voi \(\lambda_i = 2\), \(\mu_i = 3\).

Thoi gian trung binh o moi trang thai: \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_i] = 1/(\lambda_i + \mu_i) = 1/5\) gio = 12 phut.

He phuong trinh (nhan ca hai ve voi 5):

\[ \begin{cases} 5\eta(1) = 1 + 3\eta(0) + 2\eta(2) = 1 + 2\eta(2), \\ 5\eta(2) = 1 + 3\eta(1) + 2\eta(3), \\ 5\eta(3) = 1 + 3\eta(2) + 2\eta(4) = 1 + 3\eta(2). \end{cases} \]

Tu PT3: \(\eta(3) = \frac{1 + 3\eta(2)}{5}\).

Thay vao PT2: \(5\eta(2) = 1 + 3\eta(1) + 2 \cdot \frac{1 + 3\eta(2)}{5}\) \(\Rightarrow 25\eta(2) = 5 + 15\eta(1) + 2 + 6\eta(2)\) \(\Rightarrow 19\eta(2) = 7 + 15\eta(1)\).

Tu PT1: \(\eta(1) = \frac{1 + 2\eta(2)}{5}\).

Thay: \(19\eta(2) = 7 + 15 \cdot \frac{1 + 2\eta(2)}{5} = 7 + 3(1 + 2\eta(2)) = 10 + 6\eta(2)\).

Suy ra: \(13\eta(2) = 10\), \(\eta(2) = 10/13\).

Khi do: \(\eta(1) = \frac{1 + 20/13}{5} = \frac{33/13}{5} = \frac{33}{65}\), \(\eta(3) = \frac{1 + 30/13}{5} = \frac{43/13}{5} = \frac{43}{65}\).

Ket qua:

\[ \eta(1) = \frac{33}{65} \approx 0.508, \quad \eta(2) = \frac{10}{13} \approx 0.769, \quad \eta(3) = \frac{43}{65} \approx 0.662. \]

(Don vi: gio, neu \(\lambda, \mu\) tinh theo gio.)

Nhan xet: \(\eta(2) > \eta(3) > \eta(1)\). Trang thai \(2\) (o giua) mat lau nhat vi no xa ca hai bien hap thu.

10. Quan he giua phan phoi dung lien tuc va roi rac

Mot cau hoi tu nhien: phan phoi dung \(\pi\) cua qua trinh lien tuc va phan phoi dung \(\hat{\pi}\) cua chuoi nhung co quan he gi?

Dinh ly: Quan he \(\pi\) va \(\hat{\pi}\)

Neu \(\pi\) la phan phoi dung cua qua trinh Markov lien tuc (tuc \(\pi Q = 0\)), va \(\hat{\pi}\) la phan phoi dung cua chuoi nhung (tuc \(\hat{\pi} \hat{P} = \hat{\pi}\)), thi:

\[ \pi_i \propto \frac{\hat{\pi}_i}{-\lambda_{i,i}}, \qquad i \in S. \]

Tuc la: \(\pi_i = \dfrac{\hat{\pi}_i / (-\lambda_{i,i})}{\sum_j \hat{\pi}_j / (-\lambda_{j,j})}\).

Y nghia truc quan: Phan phoi dung lien tuc \(\pi_i\) ty le voi ca hai yeu to:

  1. Tan suat “ghe tham” trang thai \(i\) (do bang \(\hat{\pi}_i\)).
  2. Thoi gian o lai trang thai \(i\) moi lan ghe (do bang \(1/(-\lambda_{i,i})\)).

Trang thai duoc ghe tham thuong xuyen VA giu chan lau se co \(\pi_i\) lon.

Bai tap: Qua trinh dien tin — Telegraph process

Tin hieu dien tin chuyen giua “bat” va “tat” theo chuoi Markov lien tuc hai trang thai voi toc do bat→tat la \(\alpha = 2\) va tat→bat la \(\beta = 3\).

  1. Tim xac suat tin hieu dang “bat” tai thoi diem \(t\), biet ban dau “bat”.
  2. Tim phan phoi gioi han. Bao nhieu phan tram thoi gian tin hieu “bat”?
  3. Thoi gian trung binh mot chu ky “bat” keo dai bao lau?

Bai tap: Xac suat hap thu

Xet qua trinh sinh-tu tren \(\{0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}\) voi \(\lambda_i = 1\) va \(\mu_i = 2\) cho \(1 \leq i \leq 4\), trang thai \(0\) va \(5\) hap thu.

  1. Tinh xac suat bi hap thu vao trang thai \(0\) xuat phat tu \(i = 1, 2, 3, 4\).
  2. So sanh voi truong hop \(\lambda_i = \mu_i = 1\) (toc do bang nhau).

Bai tap: Qua trinh Yule — Yule process

Qua trinh Yule la qua trinh sinh thuan tuy voi \(\lambda_i = i\lambda\) (toc do sinh ty le voi so ca the hien tai). Bat dau voi \(X_0 = 1\).

  1. Tinh thoi gian trung binh o trang thai \(i\) (tuc \(\mathbb{E}[\tau_i]\)).
  2. Tinh thoi gian trung binh de quan the tang tu \(1\) len \(n\) ca the.
  3. Giai thich tai sao qua trinh Yule mo hinh hoa su tang truong dan so.

Bai tap: Thoi gian hap thu trung binh

Xet qua trinh sinh-tu tren \(\{0, 1, 2, 3\}\) voi \(\lambda_i = 1\), \(\mu_i = 1\) cho \(i = 1, 2\), trang thai \(0\) va \(3\) hap thu.

  1. Tinh thoi gian hap thu trung binh \(\eta(1)\) va \(\eta(2)\).
  2. So sanh voi thoi gian hap thu trong chuoi roi rac tuong ung (gambler's ruin voi \(p = q = 1/2\), \(N = 3\)).
