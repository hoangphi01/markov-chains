Ky hieu chinh

\(P_{i,j}\)
Xac suat chuyen tu \(i\) (hang) sang \(j\) (cot)
Tong hang
\(\sum_{j} P_{i,j} = 1\)
Tri rieng (2 trang thai)
\(\lambda_1 = 1\), \(\lambda_2 = 1 - a - b\)
Phan phoi gioi han
\(\pi_0 = \frac{b}{a+b}\), \(\pi_1 = \frac{a}{a+b}\)
Chapman--Kolmogorov
\(P^{m+n}_{i,j} = \sum_{l} P^m_{i,l} P^n_{l,j}\)

Ghi nho nhanh

  • Hang = xuat phat, cot = dich den
  • \(P^k\) cho xac suat sau \(k\) buoc
  • Hoi tu nhanh khi \(|1-a-b|\) nho
  • Trang thai hap thu: \(P_{k,k} = 1\)
