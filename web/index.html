Gioi thieu / About

Tai lieu nay trinh bay ly thuyet chuoi Markov bang song ngu Viet--Anh, duoc bien soan tu sach Notes on Markov Chains cua Nicolas Privault (NTU, 2011). Noi dung bao gom 5 chuong chinh va 1 phu luc, voi cac vi du chi tiet, bai tap, va phuong phap su pham giup hieu truc quan cac khai niem toan hoc.

Cac ky hieu chinh / Key Symbols

  • \(\PP\) -- Xac suat (Probability)
  • \(\E\) -- Ky vong (Expectation)
  • \(P = [P_{i,j}]\) -- Ma tran chuyen (Transition matrix)
  • \(\pi\) -- Phan phoi dung (Stationary distribution)
  • \(Q = [\lambda_{i,j}]\) -- Ma tran sinh (Generator matrix)
  • \(\lambda, \mu\) -- Toc do sinh/tu (Birth/death rates)
  • \(\tau_j\) -- Thoi gian cham/quay lai (Hitting/return time)
